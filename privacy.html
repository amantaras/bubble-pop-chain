Bubblit! Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Bubblit! is a puzzle game by Agustin Mantaras. This policy explains what the game stores or may collect when you play the web, PWA, Android, or iOS version.

Data Stored On Your Device

The game stores progress locally on your device, including level progress, coins, settings, unlocked themes, power-ups, pets, gems, achievements, quests, daily rewards, and an in-progress campaign board. On the web/PWA this uses your browser's localStorage under the key bpc_save_v1. This save data stays on your device and is not uploaded by Bubblit! to a server.

Accounts And Cloud Sync

Bubblit! does not currently create accounts and does not provide cloud save sync. Progress is local to the device/browser where you play.

Ads And Purchases

The codebase includes optional rewarded ads, interstitial ads, and in-app purchase surfaces. Store/native builds are designed to require real platform providers before paid or rewarded surfaces are presented. If real ad or purchase SDKs are enabled, their providers may process purchase receipts, ad delivery events, device identifiers, or other data needed to operate those services. Store privacy labels and this policy must be updated to match the exact SDKs enabled in the submitted build.

Analytics And Crash Reporting

Bubblit! does not currently include a third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDK. If telemetry is added later, this policy and the store privacy disclosures will be updated before release.

Children's Privacy

Bubblit! is a general-audience puzzle game. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If store settings or ad providers are configured for child-directed treatment, ad personalization should be disabled and store disclosures must reflect that configuration.

Third-Party Assets

The game includes local visual assets with attribution files in the shipped app where required. No remote asset CDN is required at runtime.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, use the support page: Bubblit! Support.
